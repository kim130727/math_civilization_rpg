# Math Civilization Puzzle Curriculum Stage Design

## Structure
- Grade: world-scale math region
- Chapter: district-scale concept arc
- Unit: mechanic-focused learning segment
- Level: short mobile stage

## Full Game Arc
- Grade 1: Seedlight Plains. Counting, comparing, addition, subtraction, shapes, and simple measure restore the first village.
- Grade 2: Clockwork Harbor. Larger numbers, fluency, early multiplication, time, money, and length keep the harbor running.
- Grade 3: Fraction Fields. Arrays, sharing, fractions, perimeter, units, and tables organize the farmland.
- Grade 4: Decimal Observatory. Large numbers, multi-digit operations, fractions/decimals, angles, area, graphs, and patterns rebuild the observatory.
- Grade 5: Ratio Reservoir. Fractions, decimals, divisors, multiples, volume, average, and data stabilize the reservoir.
- Grade 6: Skycity of Proportion. Fraction division, ratio, rate, percentage, circle area, solids, average, and probability restore the final city.

## Chapter Pattern
1. Introduction
2. Guided discovery
3. Practice
4. Variation
5. Challenge
6. Reward/restoration beat
7. New twist
8. Applied practice
9. Mixed challenge
10. Boss/mastery

## Match-3 Native Objective Families
- Collect a target value.
- Reach a visible total by matching values.
- Reduce to a target difference.
- Build equal groups or arrays.
- Split values evenly across goals.
- Assemble fractions into one whole.
- Fill decimal or percent meters.
- Clear geometry-shaped regions.
- Satisfy graph, pattern, or probability targets.
